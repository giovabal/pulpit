Database maintenance

Run engine-specific routines to reclaim disk space and refresh the query planner's statistics. Use this when the database has grown noticeably on disk or when queries feel slower than usual — refreshed statistics help Django pick better query plans, and a rebuild reclaims pages left fragmented by deletions and updates. VACUUM on SQLite holds an exclusive lock and rewrites the entire file, so it can take minutes on a multi-GB database — schedule it when crawl and analysis are idle. PostgreSQL's VACUUM ANALYZE is concurrent-safe.

Purge out-of-target messages

Delete messages — and their on-disk media — for channels that are not part of the crawl scope. A channel is kept if (a) its organisation is in target or it is marked to inspect, or (b) it is a forward source for at least one in-target message. Lost or private in-target channels are always kept; their messages are part of your data, not crawler waste. Run database VACUUM above afterwards to reclaim the freed pages on disk.
