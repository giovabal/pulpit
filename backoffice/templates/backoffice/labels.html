Labels

Labels live in groups. A partition group holds at most one label per channel at any moment (like the former Organization), so its periods can't overlap; non-partition groups allow concurrent labels. The single primary group supplies node colour, the “Label” export column, the vacancy actor identity, and the default community / role basis.
