        Pulpit project

        Pulpit (Political Undercurrents: Linkage, Propagation, Influence on Telegram) is an open-source tool that crawls Telegram channels, maps the relationships between them through forwards and t.me/ references, and produces an interactive network map — designed for political and social network analysis.
