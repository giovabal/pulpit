Similarity of channels' measure profiles — the cosine between their vectors of computed network measures (min-max normalised per measure). This is a similarity of centrality fingerprints, not structural equivalence in the Lorrain & White sense: two channels can score 1.0 here while sharing no neighbours, as long as their centrality profiles match.
